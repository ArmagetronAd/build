This is a release candidate for Armagetron Advanced 0.2.8.3, which
is planned to be our final release in the 0.2.8 and 0.2 series, apart
from bugfixes and security updates, of course. 0.2.8.3 rounds the
features of the 0.2 development series off with additions like the
new authentication system for players, rendering performance improvements
and better team management in the game.
